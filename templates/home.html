Welcome to CyberSecure

CyberSecure is a powerful honeypot simulator designed to help students, ethical hackers, and educators explore real-world cyber threats in a legal and safe environment. Learn how malicious actors operate and how to defend against them.

🛡️ Simulated Attacks

Logs threats like SQL Injection, XSS, and Directory Traversal to mimic realistic scenarios.

📊 Real-time Dashboard

Visual graphs and data logs help understand what kind of threats are being detected and from where.

📨 Email Alerts

Instantly notifies the administrator when suspicious activity is captured by the honeypot.

🎯 Threat Patterns

Detects and highlights common payloads used by attackers to exploit systems.

🔐 Cybersecurity Education

Perfect for learning how to spot and mitigate digital threats in hands-on workshops or labs.

📁 Log Export

Export captured data into CSV for offline analysis, reporting, or training exercises.
